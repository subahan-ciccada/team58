GLOBAL

WARMING

Global warming is the long-term heating of Earth's surface observed since
the pre-industrial period (between 1850 and 1900) due to human activities,
primarily fossil fuel burning, which increases heat-trapping greenhouse
gas levels in Earth's atmosphere. This term is not interchangeable
with the term "climate change."
